Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is this website free to use?
Yes — viewing and making professor reviews is completely free. We also offer optional paid features such as access to detailed subject notes and study resources.
📖 What do I get if I upgrade to the Subject Page?
Upgrading gives you access to premium features, including:
✅ Curated subject notes
✅ Study guides & reviewer materials
✅ Tips from students who previously took the class
✅ Access to exclusive future academic tools & updates
💳 How do I know I will get access after paying?
We use trusted and secure Philippine payment providers like PayMongo to process payments. Access to your purchased pages is automatically unlocked after payment — no manual waiting or approval needed.
🏫 Is this an official university website?
No. We are an independent platform created by students to help fellow students choose professors and prepare better for their subjects. We are not affiliated with DLSU, Ateneo, UP, or Benilde.
🔒 Is this website safe?
Yes. We use secure login and authentication systems such as Google OAuth to protect user accounts and ensure your data remains private.
